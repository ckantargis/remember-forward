The Patient Message

A complete open-source system of laser-engraver-ready nickel plate designs for time capsules intended to preserve human knowledge through catastrophic events — readable by a finder 200 to 2,000 years from now with no shared language or cultural context.

The Plates

The Patient Message

Each plate is a laser-engraver-ready SVG file, 480×680px portrait format, designed for nickel or stainless steel. Every plate uses only black strokes — no fills, no color — optimized for maximum legibility at any scale and any future reading technology from unaided eye to microscope.

The complete set spans eleven knowledge plates covering survival through metaphysics, and four plates per language across fifty human languages — 214 plates total plus analog audio discs.

The Capsule Designs

Seven Container Tiers

Any plate set fits any container. The tiers describe survivability and cost, not content. From a clay jar fired with a wood fire to a lead-lined granite vault — every design is fully documented and free to build.

Tier 0

Fired Ceramic + Pitch

$10 – $30

  • Clay, fire, and pine pitch only
  • No industrial materials required
  • Buildable post-catastrophe
  • Dead Sea Scroll precedent: 2,000 years

500 – 2,000 years

Tier A

Schedule 80 PVC

$75 – $150

  • Hardware store build
  • Teflon tape + marine epoxy seal
  • Silica gel + oxygen absorbers
  • Maximum household distribution

500 – 1,000 years

Tier B

Titanium Sphere

$350 – $600

  • Grade 5 Ti-6Al-4V alloy
  • Argon atmosphere fill
  • Viton O-rings, aerogel insulation
  • Pyramid cavity at Giza angle

10,000+ years

Tier C

Ocean Capsule

$400 – $900

  • HDPE hull + zinc sacrificial anodes
  • Titanium pressure vessel, 600 atm
  • Gold-plated plates only
  • Unreachable, unjurisdictable

2,000+ years at depth

Tier D

Hastelloy C-276

$600 – $1,200

  • Superalloy for acid environments
  • Volcanic, sulfurous, industrial soils
  • Outperforms titanium in corrosive zones
  • Nuclear-grade chemical resistance

10,000+ years

Tier E

Lead-Lined Granite

$500 – $1,500

  • Carved granite box, lead-poured seal
  • Fixed institutional archive
  • Nuclear industry permanence standard
  • Inscribe the bridge phrase in the stone

100,000+ years

Tier F

Mountain Archive

$2,000+

  • Dry cave or lava tube
  • Natural environmental sealing
  • Community maintenance agreement
  • GPS + surface marker required

Geological timescale

The Ten Concords

Universal Principles

Distilled from the convergence points of human civilizations across all recorded history. These are points of agreement, not imposition — discovered independently by people who had no contact with each other, across millennia. Their convergence is the evidence for their validity.

I

Every person has inherent worth.

II

Measure civilization by its most vulnerable.

III

The living world is not a resource.

IV

Knowledge is a commons.

V

Power must be held lightly.

VI

Cooperation is stronger than competition.

VII

The future has no voice. Speak for it.

VIII

Beauty is not decoration.

IX

Grief is the price of love. Pay it.

X

You are not the first. You will not be the last.
